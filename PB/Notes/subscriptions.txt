Subscription
* Represents a subscription type
* Fields
    * name: CharField
        - the name of the subscription
    * description: TextField
        - the description of the subscription
    * price: Decimal
        - the price of the subscription
        - A Decimal datatype in Python represents exact numbers, in real
        life similar datatypes are used for monetary transactions to
        prevent floating point precision errors when making monetary
        exchanges.
    * duration: Duration
        - the duration of the subscription.
